Our deps are concatenated by a gulp script at build time. See this boilerplate: https://github.com/enaukkarinen/modest-tsc-ng1-boilerplate/blob/master/gulp/tasks/vendor.js

If you use several “micro libraries” from npm, this approach is not applicable and you are indeed stuck at using CJS. As a semi-joke, I actually created a library for that: https://github.com/vivainio/decjs
